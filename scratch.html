Essential Fishing Gear

If you're just getting started, here is a breakdown of what you need to pack in your tackle box.

  1. Rod and Reel Setup
    • Spinning combo (best for beginners)
    • Fly rod (best for rivers/streams)
  2. Terminal Tackle
    • Hooks (Sizes 4 to 8 are versatile)
    • Sinkers/Weights
    • Brightly colored bobbers
  3. Live or Artificial Bait
    • Nightcrawlers/Worms
    • Plastic soft-baits or spinners
